About Northgate Resort

Where Luxury
Meets the Wild

Service, design and simplicity are at our core. From the moment you walk through our gates, you'll feel the special Northgate Resort hospitality — blissful gardens, a relaxing Olympic-size pool, and exceptional design set the scene for an extraordinary stay.

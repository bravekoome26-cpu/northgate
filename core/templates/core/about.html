About the Resort

A sanctuary for travellers seeking thoughtful luxury in northern Kenya.

Northgate Resort Isiolo is built around a simple promise: deliver warm hospitality, comfortable accommodation and convenient access to the great attractions of Kenya's Northern Frontier. Our resort blends modern comfort with local character, creating a welcoming base for both safari adventurers and business travellers.

Our Story
Family-owned, guest-first service

Founded by hospitality professionals with deep local knowledge, Northgate Resort opened in 2020 to offer clean, stylish lodging within easy reach of Isiolo International Airport and the region's top reserves.

Our resort sits on a peaceful 10-acre estate, thoughtfully designed to deliver calm, green spaces and beautifully maintained gardens while still keeping travel simple and effortless.

Why Northgate

Carefully crafted hospitality with a local heart.

From arrival to departure, our team is focused on making every stay comfortable and memorable. We provide a calm retreat, reliable services and easy access to the region's top attractions.

Convenient Location

Directly opposite Isiolo International Airport and only 2km from town.

Designed for comfort

Luxury tents and cottage rooms with modern amenities, private bathrooms and quiet outdoor areas.

What We Offer

Comfort, convenience and quiet luxury.

Accommodation

Luxury tented campsites and elegant cottage rooms are both curated to offer restful stays, spacious layouts and a sense of privacy in lush gardens.

Dining & Events

Our restaurant, bar and event facilities support everything from intimate dinners to corporate meetings and wedding celebrations.

Activities

Guests can enjoy the Olympic pool, guided nature walks, nearby game drives and family experiences accessible from the resort.

Local Connections

We partner with local suppliers and guides to bring guests a genuine northern Kenya experience with sustainable touches.

Our Promise

Reliable comfort backed by warm service.

We believe great hospitality is built on attention to detail: clean rooms, friendly staff, a well-kept pool, consistent dining and easy booking. Whether you are travelling for leisure or business, our team stands ready to make your stay smooth and memorable.
